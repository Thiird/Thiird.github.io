## Dealing with people

Living among people, we share the planet but not the world.

Awareness of this is important.
Lack of it divides us.

Trying to understand is crucial.
Listening is more important than speaking.

Not all worlds are created equal, though.
Boundaries must be in place.
Excess compassion harms more than none.
